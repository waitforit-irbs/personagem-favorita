IRON MAN

About

Tony Stark starts out in the MCU as a genius, billionaire, playboy, philanthropist with a bad personality and a armer manufacturing company.
As the story plays out, and the movies stack up, he takes major steps in his journey of self-love and responsability, caring more and more about his legacy. This is one of the reasons he stays in the Avengers Team, even though his entrance was manly to show how powerfull he was.
